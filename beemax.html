About Beemax

The Story Behind the Sensation

Beemax is the brand behind one of the most viral food trends in recent history — the Dubai chocolate bar. Combining premium Belgian chocolate with the finest Middle Eastern ingredients, Beemax has captured the imagination of food lovers worldwide.

The secret is in the filling — hand-harvested pistachios, crispy kunafa strands and golden kadaifi pastry, all encased in smooth Belgian milk or white chocolate. Every bite delivers a unique combination of textures and flavours that keeps customers coming back.

Already a bestseller on Amazon US and distributed across North America, Beemax is now making its mark in the UK — and Golden Bites Confection Co. is proud to be the official UK agent.
