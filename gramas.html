About Gramas

50+ Years of Premium Quality

Gramas is one of Turkey's most respected dried fruit and nut producers, with more than half a century of experience. Based in Germencik, Aydın — the heart of Turkey's finest fruit-growing region — Gramas controls every step from farm to consumer.

Turkey is one of the world's largest producers of dried apricots, figs, hazelnuts, pistachios and mulberries. Gramas harnesses Turkey's abundant sunshine, dry Mediterranean climate and fertile soil to deliver products of exceptional quality.

Their commitment to sustainability, organic farming and responsible production makes them a brand that today's health-conscious consumers trust.
